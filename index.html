RULES

1. Each player, starting with "Player 1" Rolls a Dice to aquire points.
2. Points are determined from the numbers on the dice rolled
3. A player may roll as much as they can to gain points
4. Points can be saved by clicking "Hold"
5. If the Number "1" is rolled all points unsaved will be lost
6. The first player to reach 100 points WINS
